### The Demographic Localizer (Content Customization)

* **The Business Pain:** A property risk management company explicitly states that *“A rescue plan is useless if the residents don’t know its contents.”* However, a generic 50-page digital PDF plan gets a near-zero open rate from apartment tenants. 
  
  Furthermore, apartment buildings have highly diverse tenant demographics (students, elderly residents, international tech workers who do not speak Finnish).
* **The AI Agent's Job:** This agent acts as a localized content transformer. It ingests the building's core, legally sound rescue plan. Based on anonymized demographic metadata provided by the housing board (e.g., *"This building in Espoo has a 40% English-speaking student population"*), the agent re-architects the delivery of the safety data. It chunk-parses the document into hyper-targeted micro-content: short multilingual scripts, simple safety checklists, or localized push notifications.
* **The Downstream Script:** The script sends these bite-sized translations directly to tenant communication channels (e.g., apartment notice-board screens, digital resident portals).
* **The Commercial Value:** Dramatically boosts tenant engagement (safety compliance), which allows the company to prove to insurance companies and housing boards that their platform actually lowers property risk, commanding a premium subscription fee.
